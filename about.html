About FreshBloom Garden Services

Your trusted partner for professional gardening and landscaping services in New York.

Our Story

Founded in 2009, FreshBloom Garden Services began with a simple mission: to help homeowners and businesses in New York create and maintain beautiful outdoor spaces that they can be proud of.

What started as a small team of passionate gardeners has grown into one of New York's most trusted landscaping services. We've completed thousands of projects, from small residential gardens to large commercial properties, always maintaining our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction.

Today, we continue to serve the New York area with the same dedication and attention to detail that has made us a household name in garden care and landscaping.

Our Mission

Our mission is to help homeowners and businesses create beautiful, healthy outdoor spaces that enhance their quality of life and property value. We achieve this through expert gardening services, sustainable practices, and unwavering commitment to customer satisfaction.
