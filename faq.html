Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our gardening services

We offer a comprehensive range of professional gardening services including:

  • Lawn Care & Maintenance (mowing, edging, fertilization)
  • Garden Design & Installation
  • Tree & Shrub Care (pruning, trimming, health assessment)
  • Irrigation System Design & Installation
  • Seasonal Cleanup (spring preparation, fall leaf removal)
  • Landscape Maintenance (ongoing care and upkeep)

Each service is customized to meet your specific needs and property requirements.

Our pricing varies based on several factors including property size, service type, and frequency. We provide customized quotes after an initial consultation where we can assess your specific needs. Contact us for a free, no-obligation estimate tailored to your property.

We offer both one-time services and recurring maintenance plans. Whether you need a single seasonal cleanup, a one-time garden installation, or ongoing weekly/bi-weekly lawn care, we can accommodate your needs.
